Training Architecture



For this architecture, we tried a number of different standard machine learning algorithms, and tried some voting methods between the highest performing ones too.
The data would first be streamed into a pre-processing. This is standard NLP preprocessing steps like stop word removal and and lemmatisation.
Then for feature representation too, we went through and tried a variety of techniques like DoctoVec, WordtoVec and weighted averaging between Text content and Title content. We also did PCA on vectors after Tf-IDF vectorisation. We did hyper parameter tuing on these different feature representations.

For Classification we tried the following algorithms:

  • SVM
  • Random Forest
  • Logistic Regression
  • XGBoost
  • Multi-Layer Perceptron

    • Dataset Used

    The dataset used has collected news from various major publications, including the New York Times, Breitbart, CNN, Business Insider, the Atlantic etc. The data primarily falls between the years of 2016 and July 2017. It had over 140,000 articles form 15 major publications.
    For Fake News, we used another dataset that was sourced from several known fake news sources, and websites.

    The data contains various metadata about each article, such as publication source,author, date,title etc. All of this information is used during the feature extraction step.


    Total Articles

    143,000 articles from 15 American publications
